An encyclopedia of 'Aquatic life'

Name: Tiger Shark

scientific name: Galeocerdo cuvier

length: 18 feet (5.5 m)

weight: 2,000 pounds (900 kg)

weapon: teeth

found: worldwide

fact: Tiger sharks are named for the distinctive, gray vertical stripes or spots covering the sides of their bodies


Name: Immortal jellyfish

scientific name: Turritopsis dohrnii

length: 4.5 mm (0.18 inches)

lifespan: Immortal

weapon: sting

found: the Caribbean Sea (nutricula) and the Mediterranean (dohrnii).

fact: It will never die until any predator kills it.
